The problem

Veeva manages your cases.
But who's watching the clock?

Your team works hard. The data is all in Vault. But without an intelligence layer on top, you're flying blind until someone builds a spreadsheet — or a deadline slips.

01

SLA breaches discovered after the fact, not before

Cases slip past the 48-hour mark while your team is handling other work. You find out in the monthly export — not with a real-time alert 6 hours before it's too late to act.

02

Manual reporting takes days that should go to science

Someone on your team exports from Vault, cleans in Excel, and builds slides — every single month. That's a medical professional doing data entry instead of supporting HCPs or reviewing content.

03

HCP trend signals are invisible to Medical Affairs strategy

Which topics are spiking this quarter? Which products drive the most off-label questions? Your Vault holds the answers — but there's no dashboard showing them to the people who need to act on them.

What Inqura does

Two modules. Everything your Medical Affairs head needs every morning.

Inqura reads directly from your existing Veeva Vault MedInquiry via the official API. No migration, no duplicate entry, no retraining your team.

M1

Inquiry Intelligence Dashboard

The view your CMO is about to ask for — already built, updated every hour from your Vault data.

  • Monthly inquiry volume trend — 12-month bar chart
  • Topic breakdown — top 7 topics sorted by volume
  • Channel split: CRM field / email / phone / web
  • HCP specialty patterns — privacy-safe, no names stored
  • Per-product inquiry split — up to 8 products by volume
  • Live case queue — 20 most recent, with SLA status per row
M2

SLA & Compliance Monitor

Live countdown timers for every open case. Breach prevented, not discovered.

  • Real-time SLA countdown per case — updates every minute
  • Breach risk queue sorted by urgency
  • One-click escalation to Slack or Microsoft Teams
  • Per-product SLA compliance % with period-over-period trend
  • 30-day compliance trend chart with 95% reference line
  • On-demand compliance PDF — one-click generation, audit-ready
